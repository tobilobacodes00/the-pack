# Parking lot

> Rule 1 (the Constitution): **The PRD is the contract.** If a feature is not in the P0 or
> P1 tables, it is automatically **P2** — one line on the roadmap slide, **zero lines of
> code**. New ideas go here, not into the sprint. Our ideation energy is the #1 internal
> threat after June 10 (risk R2). Park it; keep building.

Reopening a locked decision costs the re-opener a round of suya for the whole team
(Doc 05 §04).

## How to use this

Add a one-line entry. Don't discuss it in the sprint. Triage happens at the weekly review,
not mid-hunt. Anything here is P2 until the PRD says otherwise.
